Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and complete — your privacy matters to us.

Developer: Aelvra Last Updated: March 28, 2026
01

Data Collection

✓
ArixQ Calc does not collect any personal information. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking of any kind.

This application was built with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from your device to our servers — because we have none.

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No account or login required
  • No personal data transmitted to any server
02

Local Storage

All data generated by your use of the app — such as calculation history and your personal preferences — is stored only on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us.

You can clear this data at any time from within the app. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently and irreversibly deleted. We have no copy of it and cannot recover it.

03

Internet Usage

ArixQ Calc uses an internet connection solely to fetch live currency exchange rates from publicly available sources. This is the only network activity the app performs.

When your device makes a request to fetch these rates, your IP address is technically visible to the rate provider as part of standard internet communication — this is inherent to how the internet works and is not a data collection practice on our part. We do not log, store, or process any such information.

ℹ
Currency rates are fetched for conversion purposes only and are not financial advice. Rates may not reflect real-time market conditions.
04

Children's Privacy

ArixQ Calc does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

05

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
