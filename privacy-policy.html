Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2025
Welcome to Mayfair. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application for food ordering, takeaway, and dine-in services.
01

Information We Collect

When you use Mayfair, we collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Full name
Phone number
Email address
Delivery address
Order history
Payment transaction ID
Dine-in booking details
Device FCM token
Food preferences
App usage data
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve the Mayfair app. Specifically, we use your data to:

Process your food orders (delivery, takeaway, and dine-in), send real-time order status notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), verify your identity during order pickup and delivery using OTP, manage your saved delivery addresses and preferences, calculate delivery charges and process payments through Razorpay, and provide customer support when needed.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

03

Payment Information

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store your card details, UPI credentials, or any sensitive payment information on our servers. Only the payment transaction ID is stored to track and verify your order payment status.

Razorpay's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data. We encourage you to review it at razorpay.com/privacy.

04

Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you order status updates — such as order confirmed, food ready, out for delivery, and delivered notifications. Your device token is stored securely and is used exclusively for sending order-related notifications.

You may disable push notifications at any time through your device settings. This will not affect your ability to place orders, but you will not receive real-time order updates.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest and in transit. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and all sensitive operations require authentication.

06

Order Types & Specific Data

Depending on the type of order you place, we collect additional information:

Delivery orders — We collect your delivery address and landmark to fulfill the order. A one-time delivery OTP is generated to verify successful delivery.

Takeaway orders — We generate a pickup OTP which is used by our restaurant staff to confirm your collection. No location data is required.

Dine-in bookings — We collect your preferred visit date, time, and number of guests to manage your table reservation.

07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide our services. Order history is retained for accounting and dispute resolution purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

OTP codes are cleared from our database immediately after successful verification.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

You may update your profile information (name, phone, saved addresses) directly within the app at any time.

09

Children's Privacy

Mayfair is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via the email address associated with your account.

Continued use of the Mayfair app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us.
